WWRY:R

Perform Queen's hit with a group of players. Everyone can choose a part of the song.

Hold the device sidewards like this:

Drums: Strike with the device down for the kick and left for the snare.

Voice Solo: Strike in any direction for each beat of the verse.

Choirs: Strike left-right-left-right-up-down to perform the we-will-we-will-rock-you choirs.

‘Sing it’: Strike in any direction to fill in some voice samples.

Power Chord: Hold the device vertically up, turn it slowly into the horizontal, and suddenly turn it up side down to perform the power chord introducing the guitar solo on the last "rock you".

Guitar Riff: Strike up and down like playing a guitar to perform the guitar riff at the end of the song.

“We Will Rock You” is a song written by Brian May. It was recorded and performed by Queen for the album “News of the World”, published by EMI in 1977.
